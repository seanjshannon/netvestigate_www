SECTION 02 / Approach

The method behind every engagement.

i.

Intake

We start where most firms hand you a statement of work — by reading the room. Engagements begin with a private intake to understand what you actually fear, not what your last vendor sold you.

ii.

Reconnaissance

External and internal reconnaissance grounded in your real attack surface, your real adversaries, and your real internal politics. Documented like evidence, not marketing.

iii.

Analysis

Findings are weighed against current literature, our own corpus, and the operator reality of your team. We rank by exploitability and consequence — not severity scores.

iv.

Brief

You receive a written brief that survives a hostile reader. Engineers get the technical record; leadership gets the decision. Both versions agree.
